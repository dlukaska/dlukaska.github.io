John K. Lukaska

 

February 4, 1950 ~ April 27, 2011

My dad and I would often talk about the greatest minds of history. Tops on the list was always Isaac Newton and Nikola Tesla. The above quote is what I was able to craft at the time of my dad’s passing for the newspaper obituary. I know that he would have recognized the reference to Newton immediately and it is my way of letting him know that he was the biggest giant of them all.


I love you dad.


~ David

“John was a giant upon whose shoulders many will stand. He left the world a better place and was loved by many.”
